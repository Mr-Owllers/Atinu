What is Atinú??

Atinú is a conlang i made for daily use and literature (like poetry!)
it takes inspiration from many languages, including but not limited to:
- Proto-Indo-European
- Latin
- Proto-Germanic
- Japanese
- etc? (i forgor :skull:)

Why did I make Atinú?

like i said, i made Atinú for daily use and literature. i wanted to make a language that was easy to learn and use, but also had a lot of depth and complexity to it.
i also wanted to make a language that was unique and interesting, and that i could use to express myself in ways that i couldn't with other languages. :D
